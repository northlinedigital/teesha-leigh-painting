About

Painting with heart in Northern Ontario

Teesha Leigh Painting is a local, woman-owned painting business based in Noelville, Ontario. We service the French River and West Nipissing communities with professional residential painting.

We believe your home should reflect who you are. Whether it's a soft neutral for a cozy living room or a bold pink for a little girl's bedroom — every colour choice matters, and we treat every project with the same care we'd give our own home.
