About

Painting with heart in Northern Ontario.

Teesha Leigh Painting is a local, woman-owned residential painting business based in Noelville. We service French River, West Nipissing, and the cottages, family homes, and small business spaces in between.

Your home should reflect who you are. A soft neutral for a quiet living room. A bold pink for a little girl's bedroom. We treat every project with the care we'd give our own home.
